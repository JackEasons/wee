Wee is a lightweight, extensible bootstrap for rapidly building responsive, semantic web projects.

Quickly build wireframes, prototypes, websites and applications. It's fast, fluid, and free!

More Information

The custom.less file will serve as your primary stylesheet. Wee also includes variables.less which sets up your configuration, responsive.less which silently works trigger your preakpoints breakpoints, and wee.less which gives you boilerplate to leverage and extend. You should also configure the applicable breakpoint files. Visit weecss.com for more info.
