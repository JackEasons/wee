More Information

The style.less file inside the wee directory will serve as your primary stylesheet. There you'll also find variables.less which sets up your configuration, mixins.less for your custom LESS mixins, and ie.less for special legacy IE rules, should you need them. You should also configure the applicable breakpoint files. Visit weepower.com for more examples and documentation.
